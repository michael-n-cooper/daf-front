Room for use

As a user with reach or mobility issues, I need to have adequate room to approach a device that fits myself and any assistive technology I use (such as mobility devices).

Notes

There are also provisions for approach and clearance for wheelchairs, knees, and toes in the ADAAG and EN 301 549.
