Room for use

For stationary or installed systems, people may need adequate room to approach a device that fits them and any assistive technology they use (such as mobility devices).

Comment on this item

Notes

There are also provisions for approach and clearance for wheelchairs, knees, and toes in the ADAAG and EN 301 549.
