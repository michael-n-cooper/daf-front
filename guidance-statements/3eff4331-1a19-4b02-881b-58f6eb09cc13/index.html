Separate identification & activation

As someone who has a disability, I need to have separate means of identifying and then activating controls.

Notes

Note: This seems to be a need that everbody has, but systems are designed so controls are mostly identifiable to people without disabilities. Where labels are incorrect or with physical hardware, it might be harder for blind people to identify controls.
