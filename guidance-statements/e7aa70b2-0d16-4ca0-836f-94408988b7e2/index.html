Distinguish auditory components

As a user who is listening to multiple auditory components or channels (potentially including assistive technology or accessibility features), I need to be able to clearly perceive and distingish the various auditory components and not have them interfere with each other.

Notes

Examples:

  • Audio descriptions happening in gaps in dialog
  • Audio descriptions made with a voice distinct from other content
  • Background music can be turned off or reduced in volume so it does not interfere
