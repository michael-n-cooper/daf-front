No electromagnetic harm

As a person who is sensitive to some forms of electromagnetic radiation, I need to have systems that do not give off inappropriate electromagnetic radiation.

Notes

Note that electromagnetic hypersensitivity (EHS) is not a recognized medical diagnosis, although there are some disaiblity grants in some countries for shielding/abatement.
