Reset accessibility functions

For systems with assistive technology built into the platform or that work with assistive technology, people may need a way to reset accessibility functions to their initial states.

Notes

For example:

  • This is particularly important to be done automatically on public devices (reset volume, zoom, etc. at end of session)
