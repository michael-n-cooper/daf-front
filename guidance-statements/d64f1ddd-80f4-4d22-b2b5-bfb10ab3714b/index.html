Olfactory equivalents

For systems with olfactory or gustatory content, people may need that content presented programmatically or need equivalent visual, auditory, and tactile versions.

Notes

Obrist, M., Tuch, A. N.,

& Hornbaek, K. (2014, April). Opportunities for odor: experiences with smell and implications for technology. In Proceedings of the SIGCHI Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems (pp. 2843-2852).
