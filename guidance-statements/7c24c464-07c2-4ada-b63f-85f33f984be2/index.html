Tactile symbol clarity

As someone who feels tactile symbols, I need to have tactile symbols with an appropriate level of clarity so that I can easily perceive, differentiate, or read them.

Notes

Clarity here is often a function of the edge radius or

" sharpness " of a tactile feature (and height to a lesser extent).
