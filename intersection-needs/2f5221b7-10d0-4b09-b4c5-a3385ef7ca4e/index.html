Intersection need: No vision and No hearing

This is an intersection of the functional needs No vision and No hearing.

There are 3 guidance statements that support this intersection need:

  • No vision and no hearing equivalents
  • Tactile text speed
  • Nonvisual and Auditory Alternate Intruction References
